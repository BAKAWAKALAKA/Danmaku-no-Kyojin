    Presentation

    Danmaku no Kyojin (弾幕の巨人) is a little game that mix Touhou Project (for danmaku aspect), Warning Forever (boss waves only) and Geometry Wars (graphical feature).

    In this game, you control a ship and must survive as long as possible against waves of bosses.

    If your ship is destroyed by an enemy or if the timer reaches 0, there is a game over and the your score is transformed into credits.

    Thanks to these credits, you can buy some improvement for your ship like a better shoot frequency. You can even unlock new abilities !

    If you have a gamepad controller, you can play with a friend to crush even more enemies !

    Danmaku no Kyojin is free and open source and you already can download the first demo of the game here !

    Controls

    Keyboard + Mouse
    • Up: Z
    • Down: S
    • Left: Q
    • Right: D
    • Slow mode: Shift
    • Shoot: Left click
    • Bullet time mode: Right click
    Gamepad (Xbox 360)
    • Up: Left stick up
    • Down: Left stick down
    • Left: Left stick left
    • Right: Left stick right
    • Slow mode: LT
    • Shoot: Right stick
    • Bullet time mode: RT

    System requirement

    Windows 7 (x86 or x64) or upper with the XNA framework redistribuable 4.0 installed on it !
